## Design Context

### Users
Individual using the app to store precious memories and stay connected with loved ones. Personal use - someone who values sentimental moments, special notes, cherished songs, and shared memories.

### Brand Personality
Romantic/Intimate - warm, inviting, emotional. The interface should feel like opening a treasured photo album or reading handwritten love letters. It's personal, tender, and deeply human.

### Aesthetic Direction
Both light and dark modes supported. The dark mode should feel like a cozy evening (current), while light mode should feel like morning sunlight through windows. NOT the typical AI gradient backgrounds - instead, create something that feels handcrafted and personal.

**Tone**: Warm, tender, premium. Think "vintage polaroid gallery meets modern minimalism" - not sterile tech product.

**Differentiation**: Instead of the generic glassmorphism everywhere, create depth through layered textures. Use subtle grain/noise, intentional shadows, and warm tonal palettes that feel lived-in and human.

### Design Principles
1. **Warmth over coolness** - Colors should feel like sunset and candlelight, not neon
2. **Texture over flatness** - Add subtle grain, paper-like qualities, or handcrafted feel
3. **Intimate spacing** - Generous whitespace that feels personal, not corporate
4. **Meaningful motion** - Every animation should feel like turning a page or opening a gift
5. **Light/dark parity** - Both themes should feel intentional, not afterthoughts
